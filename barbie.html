At World of Barbie, discover, explore, and revel in her lifestyle and many careers.

Among the exciting and immersive activities, guests can:

  • Pay a visit to her iconic Dreamhouse, brought to life!
  • Chill out in her full size DreamCamper van, built by the world-famous West Coast Customs!
  • Travel to intergalactic destinations aboard Barbie Interstellar Rocket!
  • Grab the mic and record their masterpiece in Barbie Sound Studio!
  • Revisit Barbie dolls and accessories from over six decades of fun!

...And so much more!
